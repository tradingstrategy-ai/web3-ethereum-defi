## Logo variants

- **yo.logo.svg**: Black logo on transparent background - suitable for light themes (dark variant)
- **yo.logo-green.svg**: Neon green (#CCFF00) logo on transparent background - suitable for dark themes (light variant)
- **yo.icon.svg**: Square format with black background and neon green logo - can be used as favicon/app icon

## Notes

The Yo brand uses a distinctive neon green/lime colour (#CCFF00) as their primary brand colour. The logo consists of two elements: a "Y" shape on the left and a circle with cut-out on the right forming the "o".
