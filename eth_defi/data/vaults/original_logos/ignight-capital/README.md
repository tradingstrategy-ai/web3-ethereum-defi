- **Homepage**: https://ignight.capital (Wix-hosted)
- **Symbol PNG (generic)**: https://static.wixstatic.com/media/08bb3b_e06555af28ec4df690660cc120191ed6~mv2.png (white symbol on transparent, 601×601 native; fetched at 1200×1200)

## Files

- `ignight-capital.generic.png` — White symbol/brandmark on transparent background (1200×1200). Used as the primary logo since it works on both light and dark backgrounds after colour handling.
- `ignight-capital.dark.png` — White text+symbol wordmark on transparent background (800×220). Suitable for dark backgrounds.

## Notes

- The website uses Wix CMS; logos are hosted on the Wix static CDN (`static.wixstatic.com`).
- No brand kit, press page, or SVG source files were found.
- Both available assets are white-on-transparent; there is no dark/coloured variant.
- The symbol (`ignight-capital.generic.png`) is the preferred source for icon processing.
- No separate light or dark background colour variants exist; the post-processor handles inversion automatically.
