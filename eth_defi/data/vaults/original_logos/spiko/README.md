# Spiko logo source

The original assets were downloaded from the official [Spiko homepage](https://www.spiko.io/)
on 2026-07-17:

- `spiko.generic.png` is the square Spiko mark served by the official website.
- `spiko.light.png` is the official white wordmark for dark backgrounds.

Both were post-processed with `scripts/logos/post-process-logo.py` into the
standard 256×256 metadata assets. The square mark is used for the light
background variant; the white wordmark is used for the dark background variant.
