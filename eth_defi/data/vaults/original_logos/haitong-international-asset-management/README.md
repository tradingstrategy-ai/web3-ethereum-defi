# Haitong International Asset Management logo

The SVG is Asseto's hosted Guotai Haitong Securities partner logo used by the
HTCASH+ product. Guotai Haitong is the current ultimate controlling shareholder
of Haitong International Asset Management (HK) Limited following the 2025
merger. The manager's own official website currently exposes only a raster
Haitong International wordmark.
