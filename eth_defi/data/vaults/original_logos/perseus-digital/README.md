# Perseus Digital logo sources

Official Perseus Digital logo assets were extracted from the Perseus Digital homepage.

- Website: https://www.perseusdigital.io/
- Square favicon mark: https://www.perseusdigital.io/i/favicon.png
- White wordmark: https://www.perseusdigital.io/i/logo-wh.png

## Files

- `perseus-digital.generic.png` - square Perseus Digital mark from the official site favicon.
- `perseus-digital.light.png` - white Perseus Digital wordmark from the official homepage and footer.

## Notes

- Common brand-kit, media, press, assets and resources paths on the official domain returned 404.
- No official Twitter/X, LinkedIn or RSS links were found on the official homepage.
- The white transparent mark was kept as `formatted_logos/perseus-digital/light.png`.
  `generic.png` and `dark.png` use the same mark recoloured dark so it remains
  visible on light backgrounds.
