Harvest Finance API
-------------------

`Harvest Finance <https://harvest.finance/>`__ protocol integration.

Harvest is a crypto smart contract platform that optimises yield farming for its users via automated
strategies. There are hundreds of third-party platforms to accrue yields from in crypto, such as
lending, staking, or liquidity provision, and the Harvest platform aggregates the best yield
opportunities to help its users accrue more yield through automation.

Harvest offers a simplified but powerful platform to discover yield strategies across the decentralised
finance ecosystem and helps you keep track of important performance metrics. Over 100 yield strategies
for ETH, WBTC, USDC, USDT, and more are available across Ethereum Mainnet, Arbitrum, Base, Polygon
and zkSync Era.
